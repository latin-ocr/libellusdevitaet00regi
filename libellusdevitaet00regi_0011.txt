AT a MEETING of the COUNCIL of the SURTEES SOCIETY‚
on the Qssth Sept„ 1844,

REsoLvED, That, for the year 1845, the Life of ST. GODRIC,
by REGINALD, be printed by the SOCIETY, under the editorship
'of Mr. STEVENSON, on the usual terms, With such additions
from the other Lives of the Saints as the COUNCIL shall think

proper.
JAMES RAINE, Secretary.
